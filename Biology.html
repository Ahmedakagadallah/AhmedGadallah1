Due to the curricular differences between STEM schools and primary and elementary schools, many students find it challenging to adjust. Therefore, I cooperated with some students at a STEM school and conducted 5+ sessions for the newcomers to assist them learn how to study and which resources to use. In addition, we maintained a close check on them until the final exams; we created a question bank for the curriculum-based biological subjects.
